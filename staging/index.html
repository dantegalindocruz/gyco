Chatter Magazine

You may be asking yourself at this very moment, 'Who is this fabulous person behind such an awesome site?!' First off, let me just say thank you. Secondly, let me just say that I like to think that I'm pretty awesome myself. But on a more serious note, my name is Dante Galindo Cruz. Some of you may have seen me in June's issue of Chatter Magazine and some of you may have seen me walking around town knitting. So, let me tell you the tea (for those of you who don't know what I mean by the tea, in this context it means some really good info). My name is Dante Galindo Cruz, I am a fulltime crafter with 13 years of crocheting experience and just recently this year I'm officially a Norwegian knitter. What that equats to is that I'm good at what I do and I know an easier way to knit which makes pearling super fun.

That leads me to my next point which is I'm offering FREE crocheting and knititng classes every Saturday at The Camp House here in Downton Chattanooga. This will give you an opportunity to come out and finally tackle that goal of finally making that super cool beanie. Or you may know some freinds or family members in need of some cool baby blankets. Or if you're a fashionsita you can finally start making your own clothes. And with both Fall and Winter upon the horizon, now is an even better time to get your craft own. Just imagine gifting somebody a wonderful garment or handmade item that they can cherish for a lifetime. No one is too young or too old to get started on their crafting journey. All you have to do is show up and be willing to work. Check out my classes down below.
